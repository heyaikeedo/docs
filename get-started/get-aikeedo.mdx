## How to purchase?

Aikeedo licenses are available for purchase through the following official channels:

1. **Official Website:** Visit our [official website](https://aikeedo.com?utm_source=docs) to purchase directly from the source.
2. **CodeCanyon:** Aikeedo is also available on [CodeCanyon](https://codecanyon.net/item/aikeedo-ai-powered-content-platform-saas-ready/48578733), a trusted marketplace for premium software solutions.

Upon completing your purchase, you will receive:

- A ready to install Aikeedo software (source code)
- A license key for activating Aikeedo on your server

These resources will enable you to deploy and customize Aikeedo according to your specific requirements.
