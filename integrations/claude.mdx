## Introduction

Integrating Claude AI into your Aikeedo platform opens up a world of possibilities for advanced natural language processing and AI-assisted tasks. Claude is known for its strong performance in areas like content generation, coding assistance, and complex problem-solving.

## Setting Up the Integration

Follow these steps to connect Claude AI with your Aikeedo platform:

### Step 1: Obtain Your Claude AI API Key

1. If you don't have an Anthropic account, [sign up here](https://www.anthropic.com) or contact their sales team for API access.
2. Once you have access, navigate to your Anthropic dashboard.
3. Locate and copy your API keyâ€”you'll need this for Aikeedo.

<Note>
  Keep your API key secure and confidential. It grants access to Claude AI services and should not be shared publicly.
</Note>

### Step 2: Configure Aikeedo

1. Log in to your Aikeedo admin panel.
2. Navigate to **Settings > Anthropic / Claude AI**.
3. Paste your Claude AI API key into the designated "API Key" field.
4. Click **Save changes** to activate the integration.

<Check>
  Great job! Claude AI is now integrated with your Aikeedo platform.
</Check>

To maximize your Claude AI integration:

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with the Claude AI integration:

1. Verify that your API key is correct and active.
2. Check your Anthropic account for any usage limits or restrictions.
3. Ensure your Aikeedo platform is up to date.
4. If you receive error messages related to model access or quotas, verify your account status with Anthropic.
5. For persistent issues, contact [Aikeedo support](mailto:support@aikeedo.com) for assistance.

  Remember to review Anthropic's usage policies and pricing to ensure compliance and manage costs effectively as you scale your Claude AI integration.
</Note>
